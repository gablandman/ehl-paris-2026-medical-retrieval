## Done — summary

**Problem solved:** dataset2 was the weak link in the MIND retrieval pipeline. ds2 applies an independent rigid+elastic deformation to query vs target, breaking the voxel correspondence that MIND's spatial mean-|.| comparison depends on (ds1/ds3 are already co-registered and were near-perfect).

**What I built:** `mind_register.py` — affine pre-registration that warps the target into the query frame *before* MIND scoring, ds2-only. Key design choice: the registration metric is **MIND distance itself**, since the modalities differ (T1 vs T2) and intensity metrics like NCC/SSD are unreliable cross-modal. A 12-param affine optimized by Adam on a downsampled grid (fast). Two strategies exposed: `pairwise` (validated) and `template`. ds1/ds3 reuse the existing `mind_sim_matrix` untouched. Plus `run_mind_reg.sh` mirroring `run_mind.sh`.

- Offline synthetic-ds2: MRR **0.52 → 1.00**, top-1 conflict **0.70 → 0.00**
